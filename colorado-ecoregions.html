One-time data setup

This page expects /data/colorado-ecoregions.geojson. Convert the EPA Level IV shapefile to WGS84 GeoJSON with mapshaper:

npx mapshaper co_eco_l4.shp \
  -proj wgs84 \
  -simplify 5% keep-shapes \
  -o format=geojson precision=0.00001 data/colorado-ecoregions.geojson

Or with GDAL:

ogr2ogr -t_srs EPSG:4326 -f GeoJSON data/colorado-ecoregions.geojson co_eco_l4.shp

If you have the national CONUS file, clip to Colorado first:

npx mapshaper us_eco_l4.shp \
  -proj wgs84 \
  -filter '"Colorado".indexOf(STATE_NAME) > -1' \
  -simplify 5% keep-shapes \
  -o data/colorado-ecoregions.geojson
