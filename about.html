About Me

Hello, I'm Justin.

I'm a software engineer / ultra-endurance athlete based out of Boulder, CO.

My Skills

I have 8 years of programming experience, with a strong knowledge of back-end control systems for industrial automation projects.

Graduating from the University of Delaware with a 3.98 GPA in Electrical Engineering, I have since written controls for the following applications:

  • Large-scale chemical plant automation in the energy industry
  • High frequency laser heterodyne stabilization in the communication industry
  • Critical environment cloud computing hardware in the technology industry

I am strongest in Python and C programming languages, but am experienced in Java, Matlab, IEC 61131-3 languages (LD, FBD, ST), C#, Javascript, HTML, and LabVIEW.

My Hobbies

When I'm not coding, my mission is to travel the world, partaking in the most grueling athletic challenges and mountain objectives on the planet.

Some of my notable accomplishments include:

  • Completing an IRONMAN triathlon in 2019
  • Completing a 100-mile ultramarathon in 2023
  • Summitting an 18,491ft volcano in 2024

These extreme endurance activities force me to stay focused and determined on my goals, which I carry over to all tasks and challenges in my professional life.
